Keep in touch with us

There are different and complementary ways for keeping in touch with us, for asking questions about our ontologies, for discussing about their possible applications, and even for starting new collaborations. In particular, SPAR Ontologies have available:

  • a Twitter account, where we publish all the news related to the SPAR Ontologies and their related works. Follow us for being informed about all the most recent news!

  • an open Google group for discussing issues and possible applications of the SPAR Ontologies with us and the whole community. Join the forum and discuss with other interested researchers!

  • a ticket collector (subscription to SourceForge is required) for highlighting issues of any of the SPAR Ontologies. Spot bugs and suggest improvements in our ontologies!

  • an email for personal and private communications with us, and for suggesting projects and publications related with SPAR Ontologies. Write us for discussing our work directly with us!

In addition, please read carefully the guidelines at https://sparontologies.github.io if you are interested in proposing a new ontology to be added within the SPAR Ontologies.
